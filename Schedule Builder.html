Students who wish to graduate in 4 years should strive to enroll in 15 units per quarter.

If you are a Cal Grant recipient, per state law - Assembly Bill No. 2248, we are required to inform you:

  1. A Cal Grant award is limited to four academic years, except as provided in California Education Code subdivision (c) of Section 69433.6.
  2. A student needs to take 15 units per semester or the equivalent quarter units, or 30 semester units or the equivalent quarter units per academic year in order to graduate within four years.*
*At UC Davis, the equivalent quarter units are 15 units per quarter or 45 units per academic year. Students are only required to be enrolled in 12 quarter units for the full Cal Grant amount to disburse each term.
